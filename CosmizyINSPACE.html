Controls

Use the arrow keys to navigate your spaceship:

  • Up Arrow or W: Move up
  • Down Arrow or S: Move down
  • Left Arrow or A: Brake
  • Right Arrow or D: Boost
Press the Spacebar to fire your weapon.

Hold R to reload your weapon.
